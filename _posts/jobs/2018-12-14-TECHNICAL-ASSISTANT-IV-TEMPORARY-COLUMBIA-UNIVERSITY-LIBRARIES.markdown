### Description   

The Columbia University Libraries seeks a temporary Technical Assistant IV. 

Duties include: 
Transport, pickup, and deliver materials between and among Columbia University Libraries and a variety of off-campus locations according to a guaranteed delivery schedule using a hand truck and/or the Libraries van. Shelve and shift books pre- and post-delivery to and from campus locations. Sort, open, and deliver incoming mail and other library materials. Wrap books and other library materials for mailing and courier services, and prepare materials for shipment using postal scales, postage meter, and UPS software. Receive and sign for UPS, Federal Express, and other delivery shipments. Receive and return materials to the ReCAP facility courier. Fulfill supply requests. Other duties as assigned and necessary. 

Monday-Friday, 9:00am-5:00pm 

THIS IS A TEMPORARY POSITION FROM DATE OF HIRE TO February 28, 2019.
